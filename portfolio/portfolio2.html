ELQELGUE - RADIO & TV

Stimulate your senses

Embark on a journey through the lively world of "El Qelgue," a radio and TV program that emerged from the creative realms of my high school days. In collaboration with talented colleagues from the "Communication and Production for the Media" course, we brought to life a series of captivating productions, seamlessly merging the captivating sound of radio with the visual enchantment of television.

Our school studio became the heart of innovation as we unleashed the magic of storytelling through the airwaves. Each episode, meticulously crafted and filled with passion, found its digital haven on YouTube, captivating audiences with our unique brand of entertainment. The combination of audio and visual elements in "El Qelgue" created an immersive experience, where every story, interview, and musical moment unfolded like a vibrant display of creativity. Dive into the archives of our Instagram page, where we shared glimpses behind the scenes, exclusive content, and engaged with our growing community of listeners. "El Qelgue" became more than a program; it became a platform for artistic expression, collaboration, and the boundless possibilities that arise when creative minds come together. Join us on this adventure of audiovisual exploration, where passion meets production, and every episode is a testament to the enduring spirit of our high school endeavor.
