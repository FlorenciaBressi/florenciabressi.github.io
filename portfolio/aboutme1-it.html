UNA - Performing Arts

Embark on a profound journey through my artistic experience at the Universidad Nacional de las Artes, where the vibrant world of performing arts unfolds. Over two enriching years, I have immersed myself in the captivating facets of acting and performing arts, exploring the intricate pathways of creative expression. From the very beginning, my journey was marked by excellence, as I excelled in the entrance exam, setting the stage for an immersive and disciplined exploration of the artistic universe.

At UNA, I have not merely pursued academic studies but have become an artist in the truest sense, delving into various aspects of the creative spectrum. From the dramatic intensity of acting to the rhythmic allure of dance, the harmonious notes of music, and the visual poetry of performance art, I have consistently shaped my artistic identity. This academic haven has been instrumental in shaping my career, fostering a deep-seated passion for visual arts, music, and communication. As a dedicated student, I have contributed to the vibrant community at UNA, engaging with my peers in collaborative projects, performances, and artistic dialogues. The university has become a nurturing ground where discipline meets creativity, and each day unfolds as a new chapter in my ongoing artistic journey. Join me as I continue to explore the boundless possibilities that the performing arts offer, navigating this adventure with passion, grace, and an unwavering commitment to artistic excellence.
