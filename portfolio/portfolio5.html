"Encontrar (nos) -
Short Film"

A Cinematic Tapestry of Fate, Emotion, and Connection

"Encontrar(nos)" is more than a film; it's a poetic exploration that unravels the invisible threads binding two persons. In this visual journey, the red thread of fate intertwines with the essence of destiny within the hallowed halls of a church. Collaborating closely with friends immersed in the world of fashion design, we crafted a story that goes beyond the screen, weaving emotions into every frame.

This project is not just about storytelling; it's an emotional odyssey. The film captures the beauty of fate and the anticipation of a destined encounter.  "Encontrar(nos)" goes beyond mere visuals; it's a heartfelt narrative that invites you to immerse yourself in a world where invisible threads come to life, connecting us in ways that resonate long after the film's conclusion. This film extends beyond mere imagery; it's a heartfelt narrative encapsulating the intricacies of connection, design, and storytelling. This collaboration weaves emotions into every frame, creating an indelible impact on the audience and solidifying its place as a significant chapter in my portfolio.
